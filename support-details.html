Support Feature Guide

Choose the support path that matches your situation right now.

Start a Talk

  1. Pick someone safe and trusted.
  2. Open with one clear line: “I need support.”
  3. Ask for one specific thing you need right now.

Find Help

  1. Check your school counseling office hours.
  2. Ask a teacher to help you connect to student support.
  3. Set one follow-up meeting before leaving.

Helpline Info

  1. If you need urgent support, call your local emergency number.
  2. Use trusted helplines from your school or local health services.
  3. For non-urgent needs, use the Contact page for follow-up support.

Practical Tips

Use these guidelines to make your routine more effective and easier to maintain.

Consistency

Keep It Small

Start with short sessions and repeat daily. Consistent short habits are more reliable than long plans done rarely.

Structure

Track One Metric

Choose one number to follow each week, such as minutes, steps, or journal entries, so progress stays visible.

Support

Check In Weekly

Share your progress with a friend, classmate, or trusted adult once a week to keep motivation steady.
