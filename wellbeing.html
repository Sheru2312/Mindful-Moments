Practical Tips

Wellbeing habits work best when they are attached to existing routines.

Sleep

Anchor Wake Time

Keep your wake time consistent, even if bedtime shifts. This stabilizes energy and attention through the day.

Hydration

Pair Water With Tasks

Take water breaks after each study session. Pairing habits with actions helps consistency without reminders.

Reflection

Write a Daily Line

One short journal sentence per day helps track mood trends and notice what supports your wellbeing most.
