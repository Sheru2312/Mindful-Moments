Focus Feature Guide

Use these tools when you need structure quickly.

Pomodoro Round

  1. Choose one task only.
  2. Set a 25 minute timer and work without switching tabs.
  3. Take a 5 minute break away from your desk.
  4. Repeat 4 rounds, then take a longer 15 minute break.

Reset Checklist

  1. Clear your workspace and close distractions.
  2. Take 3 slow breaths.
  3. Pick the easiest next action and do it now.

Practical Tips

Use these guidelines to make your routine more effective and easier to maintain.

Consistency

Keep It Small

Start with short sessions and repeat daily. Consistent short habits are more reliable than long plans done rarely.

Structure

Track One Metric

Choose one number to follow each week, such as minutes, steps, or journal entries, so progress stays visible.

Support

Check In Weekly

Share your progress with a friend, classmate, or trusted adult once a week to keep motivation steady.
