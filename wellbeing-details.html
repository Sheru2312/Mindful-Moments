Wellbeing Feature Guide

Pick a tool and use the simple steps below.

Night Routine

  1. Set a target sleep time and alarm.
  2. Put your phone away 30 minutes before bed.
  3. Write one calming thought before sleeping.

Refill Reminder

  1. Keep a water bottle near your study space.
  2. Take 4 sips every time you finish a task.
  3. Refill at lunch and before evening study.

Move Break

  1. Roll shoulders for 30 seconds.
  2. Walk around the room for 2 minutes.
  3. Do a quick stretch for your neck and back.

Box Breath

  1. Breathe in for 4 counts.
  2. Hold for 4 counts.
  3. Breathe out for 4 counts.
  4. Repeat for 4 rounds.

Daily Note

  1. Write one good thing from today.
  2. Write one hard thing you handled.
  3. Write one small goal for tomorrow.

Practical Tips

Use these guidelines to make your routine more effective and easier to maintain.

Consistency

Keep It Small

Start with short sessions and repeat daily. Consistent short habits are more reliable than long plans done rarely.

Structure

Track One Metric

Choose one number to follow each week, such as minutes, steps, or journal entries, so progress stays visible.

Support

Check In Weekly

Share your progress with a friend, classmate, or trusted adult once a week to keep motivation steady.
