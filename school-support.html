Support

Find School Support

School counselors, mentors, and student wellness staff can help with stress, planning, and immediate support options.

  • Check counseling office hours and drop-in slots.
  • Ask a teacher to connect you to student services.
  • Bring one short note with the key issue you want help with.
  • Before leaving, confirm your next follow-up date.
Tip: ask for one concrete support action today (meeting, accommodation, or resource referral).
